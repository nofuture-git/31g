 ANSI ASC X12C Communications and Controls Subcommittee Technical Report Type II
 ASC X12 REFERENCE MODEL FOR XML DESIGN
 July 2002
----

----
Contain the info
 - Who: which parties participate in the business transaction 
        and the actors involved in the exchange.
 - What: the primary subject or purpose of the message
 - When: answers event/timing details.
 - Where: answers location detials.
 - Why: typically answered by the message type itself along with
		accompanying reference inforamtion.
----
